**Rajender Kumar** is a seasoned corporate law professional with over three decades of experience in the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. He is a qualified Cost and Management Accountant (CMA), Company Secretary, and holds an LL.B. degree.

---

### Professional Career

1. **Registrar of Companies, New Delhi** – Technical scrutiny of balance sheets, composition of offences.
2. **Assistant Official Liquidator, Allahabad** – Appeared in court, handled compulsory winding‑up, taking possession and sale of assets, claims adjudication, dissolution, and voluntary winding‑up. Gained working knowledge of RDDBFI Act, 1993 and SARFAESI Act, 2002.
3. **Registrar, NCLT, Allahabad** – Dealt with all types of matters: oppression & mismanagement, reduction of capital, merger & amalgamation, change of company status, and cases under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016.
4. **Deputy Director, Office of Regional Director (Southern Region), Chennai** – Carried out inspections of companies.
5. **Dy. Registrar of Companies, CRC, Manesar** – Experience in company incorporation.
6. **Dy. Official Liquidator, Official Liquidator Office, Bombay** – Further winding‑up work.
7. **Joint Director, Ministry of Corporate Affairs** – Attained superannuation on 31.08.2025 after a distinguished career.
